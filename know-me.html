Hi, I’m Ajith Alphonse, currently pursuing my Master’s in Information Systems at the University of Utah. I focus on designing learning experiences and training systems that help people understand complex tools and workflows with clarity and confidence.

My work sits at the intersection of instructional design and user experience. I design structured learning solutions, step-by-step training, and performance support materials that reduce confusion, improve task success, and enable employees to work more efficiently.

At the University of Utah Campus Store, I work closely with NetSuite admins, developers, and business teams to identify workflow challenges and design training solutions that simplify system usage. From guided product publishing workflows to job aids and system-based learning, my goal is to turn complex operational processes into intuitive learning experiences.

My journey started with a strong foundation in design and development, but over time I became more interested in how people learn and perform in real environments. Instead of just designing interfaces, I began focusing on how to guide users, reduce cognitive load, and support them through structured learning.

I enjoy breaking down complex systems into clear, learnable steps and creating training experiences that are practical, accessible, and directly tied to real-world tasks.
